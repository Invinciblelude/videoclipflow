FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes. VideoClipFlow is designed for users to process their own content or content they have permission to use. Downloading your own videos, transcribing your own podcasts, and extracting audio from content you own is completely legal. Our Terms of Service require users to only process content they have rights to.

What sites are supported?

Over 1,000 websites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Vimeo, SoundCloud, Twitch, Facebook, and many more.

How accurate is the transcription?

We use state-of-the-art AI speech recognition (OpenAI Whisper) supporting 90+ languages. Accuracy is typically 95%+ for clear speech.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard anytime.

What file formats do you support?

Transcripts: TXT, DOCX, PDF. Captions: SRT, VTT, ASS. Audio: MP3, WAV, FLAC. Video: MP4, WebM. Clips: MP4 (9:16 vertical).

Do you store my files?

Files are available for 24 hours then automatically deleted. We never share, sell, or use your content.
