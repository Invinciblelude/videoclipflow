Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines what is and isn't allowed when using VideoClipFlow. Our goal is to provide a useful, legal, and ethical media processing service. We expect all users to use VideoClipFlow responsibly.

Permitted Uses

VideoClipFlow is designed for the following legitimate use cases:

  • Processing your own content — Transcribing, captioning, or extracting media from content you created and own.
  • Processing content you have rights to — Working with content where you have explicit written permission from the owner, a valid license, or are acting as an authorized agent.
  • Public domain content — Processing content that is in the public domain or released under Creative Commons or similar open licenses.
  • Fair use purposes — Using content for commentary, criticism, education, research, or news reporting as permitted under fair use doctrine.
  • Accessibility — Generating captions, transcripts, or alternative formats to make content accessible to people with disabilities.
  • Personal backups — Downloading your own content from platforms for personal archival purposes.

Prohibited Uses

The following uses of VideoClipFlow are strictly prohibited:

  • Copyright infringement — Downloading, copying, or distributing copyrighted content without authorization from the rights holder.
  • Piracy — Using VideoClipFlow to circumvent digital rights management (DRM) or access paywalled content you haven't paid for.
  • Mass scraping — Automated or bulk downloading of content from any platform for redistribution or commercial resale.
  • Impersonation — Processing content to create deepfakes, impersonate individuals, or misrepresent the origin of content.
  • Illegal content — Processing any content that is illegal under applicable law, including but not limited to child exploitation material, content promoting violence, or materials related to terrorism.
  • Spam or abuse — Using the Service to generate spam, engage in harassment, or facilitate any form of abuse.
  • System abuse — Attempting to overload, disrupt, or exploit the Service infrastructure, including circumventing rate limits or usage caps.
  • Reselling access — Reselling or sublicensing your VideoClipFlow account or API access without written permission.

Your Responsibility

By using VideoClipFlow, you represent and warrant that:

  • You have the legal right to process any content you submit.
  • You will comply with all applicable local, state, national, and international laws.
  • You understand that VideoClipFlow is a tool — the responsibility for how it is used rests with you.
  • You will not hold VideoClipFlow liable for any misuse of content processed through the Service.

Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this AUP without prior notice.
  • Remove or block access to content processed in violation of this policy.
  • Report illegal activity to the appropriate authorities.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement investigations when legally required.

Reporting Violations

If you believe someone is using VideoClipFlow in violation of this policy, please report it to abuse@videoclipflow.com. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Changes

We may update this AUP as needed. Continued use of VideoClipFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
